Born to engineer

I've been a tinkerer my whole life. There are photos of me when I was 5 years old, sitting behind an IBM machine running Windows 95. Growing up, I was always fascinated by technology. It seemed like actual magic that with a few clicks of a mouse you could literally have a video chat with someone sitting on the opposite side of the world, like something out of Star Trek. Also baffling, it took mankind just 57 years to go from the invention of the lightbulb, to the invention of videotelephony. The day I realized that what made all of this possible was electrical engineering I knew I had to learn more.

As an engineer, I'm an excellent problem solver. Give me a challenge and I will find you a solution. My ability to program and develop software combined with my electrical engineering background enables me to succeed in a vareity of different engineering roles.

I love technological tasks of all vareities. Whether it's purely software, purely hardware or a mixture of both.
